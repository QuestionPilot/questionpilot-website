The Problem

Structured transactions deserve a better tool.

Requiring clients and counterparties to work in error-prone spreadsheets creates friction, risk, and a poor impression at every stage of the process.

  • !Version management issues arise from back-and-forth emails across internal deal teams, sellers, and buyers, each working from their own copy of the file.
  • !No audit trail. Who responded, when, and what was shared is impossible to reconstruct cleanly after the fact.
  • !Getting answers from your client requires a separate email thread. They are not living in your tracker, and coordinating responses across parties adds significant overhead to every deal.
  • !Exchanging spreadsheets with multiple parties via email lacks security controls and creates ongoing opportunity for misdirected files and data errors.
  • !Buyers ask the same questions across multiple groups. Without a shared system, your team answers them multiple times, with no guarantee of consistency.
  • !Your process is a direct reflection of your firm. Counterparties notice when an advisor is organized, professional, and running a clean deal.
